quickFilters Change Log - Version 4.5 I am currently working on compatibility with Thunderbird beta 88.0 for preparing next years ESR period (2021/2022). As in between step I have released quickFilters 5.1 as a "mail experiment" for Thunderbird 78+. If you are interested in using a version of quickFilters that works with Thunderbird 78, buy a license to support the project.
